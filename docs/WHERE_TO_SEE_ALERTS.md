## ğŸ”” **Notification Types**

### Alert Types Generated by AI:

1. **Academic Risk Alert** (`academic_risk`)
   - **When:** Student grade < 75 in a subject
   - **Severity:** Medium or High
   - **Shows:** Subject name, current grade, reasons

2. **Overall Risk Alert** (`overall_risk`)
   - **When:** Student at risk in 2+ subjects or high overall risk
   - **Severity:** Medium or High
   - **Shows:** Number of at-risk subjects, overall risk level

3. **Attendance Alert** (`attendance`)
   - **When:** Attendance < 80%
   - **Severity:** Medium or High
   - **Shows:** Attendance percentage, days absent
